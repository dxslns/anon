What I Do

I review early-stage websites and apps with a focus on:

First Impression

First 10–30 seconds experience analysis to understand what users see and feel immediately.

Clarity of Value

Evaluating how clearly your product communicates its value and next steps for users.

Visual Trust & UI Cleanliness

Assessing visual design elements that build (or break) user trust and perception.

Mobile Usability

Testing and optimizing the mobile experience for on-the-go users.

Friction Detection

Identifying small issues that quietly kill engagement and conversion rates.

Async Process

Everything is async — no calls, no meetings, just clear, actionable feedback.

How It Works

Simple and straightforward process designed for busy founders.

1

Share Your Product

What's Included in a Full UX Audit

You'll receive a concise PDF (1–2 pages) with:

  • Clear list of UX & UI issues
  • Screenshots with explanations
  • Priority ranking (what to fix first)
  • Quick wins vs deeper improvements
  • Focus on first-time and mobile users
  • No fluff, no generic advice

Optionally, I can help fix or redesign key parts in Figma if needed.
